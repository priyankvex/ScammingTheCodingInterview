Problem 045


Two nodes in a BST are swapped. Correct the tree.


Two nodes in a BST are swapped, correct the tree.

Input Tree:
10
/ \
5 8
/ \
2 20

In the above tree, nodes 20 and 8 must be swapped to fix the tree. Following is the output tree:
10
/ \
5 20
/ \
2 8


Solution



We can leverage the fact that the inorder traversal of a BST is a sorted list of numbers.
Now since the numbers that are swapped will be smaller than their previous element, we can leverage this to get the incorrect nodes.

If the incorrect nodes are not adjacent we'll find 2 such instances where the current element is smaller than the previous element, otherwise there will be only 1 such instance.
